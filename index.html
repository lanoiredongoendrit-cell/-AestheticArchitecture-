Phase 1: Craniofacial Mechanics

The Golden Rule

Tongue should be suctioned to the roof of the mouth, not just touching it. This supports the maxilla and prevents facial sagging over time.

Nasal Dominance

Mouth breathing can contribute to poor facial development. Prioritize nasal breathing and improve sleep posture.
